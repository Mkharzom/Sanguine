Spot Leukaemia Client: Leukaemia Care UK and Leukaemia UK

Britain’s two largest leukaemia organisations Leukaemia U.K and Leukaemia Care approached us to deliver a UK-wide campaign that would raise awareness of the 3 key symptoms of leukaemia as well as drive people experiencing them to speak up. At the time, relentless Covid messaging had left the public feeling fatigued, so we knew an unconventional creative strategy would be required to divert attention to yet another set of symptoms. To generate cut-through and memorability, we built a campaign around an endearing parrot who created a catchy rap that would make the symptoms of leukaemia unforgettable. The campaign encouraged people experiencing the symptoms to emulate the parrot’s outspoken nature and parrot on about the symptoms.
