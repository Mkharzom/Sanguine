The Future of Surgery Client: New Zealand Association of General Surgeons

The New Zealand Association of General Surgeons engaged us to promote their 2022 conference about the future of surgery. We responded with a hard-hitting digital campaign that brought to life an extremely advanced artificially intelligent surgeon. The campaign launched with an animated banner in which an unsupervised simulated AI surgeon appeared to be operating on its first patient in real-time. Subsequent banners challenged surgeons to contemplate the benefits and dangers of a fully independent AI surgeon left to its own devices.
