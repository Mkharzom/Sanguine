The Future of Surgery Client: New Zealand Association of General Surgeons

The New Zealand Association of General Surgeons engaged us to promote their 2022 conference about the future of surgery. We responded with a hard-hitting digital campaign which ventured deep into the world of artificial intelligence, the most significant emerging technology in healthcare. The campaign launched with an animated banner in which a simulated artificially intelligent surgeon appeared to be operating on its first patient in real-time, on its own. Additional thought-provoking banners challenged surgeons to ponder the benefits and dangers of AI in the surgical field.
